## Purpose

This document serves as the master architectural blueprint for the Property Operating System.

It defines the complete operating model, business model, technology model, workflow model, intelligence model, compliance model, and scaling strategy.

---

## Mission

Create the most complete Property Operating System possible.

The platform should help operators:

- Manage properties
- Manage tenants
- Manage vendors
- Manage maintenance
- Manage finances
- Manage compliance
- Manage documents
- Manage communications
- Manage inspections
- Manage risk
- Make better decisions

---

## Vision

A single operating system for property ownership and management.

Not merely software.

An operational intelligence platform.
