# COMPLIANCE ENGINE 005: LEGAL DISCLAIMER MODEL

## Purpose

Defines how legal disclaimers should be handled.

## Principle

The platform may assist with compliance awareness, reminders, document organization, and workflow guidance.

The platform should not present itself as a substitute for legal counsel.

## Required Disclaimer Areas

- Eviction notices
- Rent increases
- Security deposit deductions
- Applicant denials
- Lease violations
- Legal deadlines
- Jurisdiction-specific law summaries

## Rule

High-risk legal outputs require human review.
