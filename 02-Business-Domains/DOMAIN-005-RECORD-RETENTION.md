# DOMAIN 005: RECORD RETENTION

## Purpose

Defines retention requirements for records and evidence.

## Record Categories

- Leases
- Applications
- Inspections
- Communications
- Financial Records
- Vendor Records
- Compliance Records
- Notices
- Audit Logs

## Retention Rule

Every record must have:

- Retention policy
- Retention start date
- Destruction eligibility date
- Legal hold capability
