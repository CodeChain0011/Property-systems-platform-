# DOMAIN 001: COMPLIANCE

## Purpose

The Compliance Domain ensures that every action taken by the platform aligns with applicable laws, regulations, policies, deadlines, and operational requirements.

## Major Areas

- Fair Housing
- Landlord-Tenant Law
- Security Deposits
- Rent Control
- Property Registration
- Licensing
- Insurance
- Safety Requirements
- Habitability Standards
- Accessibility Requirements
- Tax Reporting
- Vendor Compliance
- Record Retention

## Compliance Rule

Every compliance requirement must have:

- Trigger
- Responsible Party
- Deadline
- Required Documents
- Required Notifications
- Escalation Path
- Audit Record
