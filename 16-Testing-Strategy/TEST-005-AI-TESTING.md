# TEST 005: AI TESTING

## Purpose

Defines early AI testing principles.

## AI Tests

- AI cites source records when possible.
- AI does not invent legal conclusions.
- AI does not approve actions automatically.
- AI respects permissions.
- AI refuses data it cannot access.
- AI distinguishes facts from recommendations.
- AI routes high-risk decisions to human review.

## Rule

AI must be tested as a decision-support system, not as an autonomous authority.
