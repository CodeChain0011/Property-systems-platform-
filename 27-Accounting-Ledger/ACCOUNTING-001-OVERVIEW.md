# ACCOUNTING 001: OVERVIEW

## Purpose

Defines the accounting foundation for the Property Operating System.

## Goal

The platform must track income, expenses, deposits, vendor payments, owner distributions, reserves, and property-level profitability.

## Core Accounting Areas

- Rent income
- Security deposits
- Late fees
- Utility reimbursements
- Vendor expenses
- Repairs
- Capital expenditures
- Mortgage payments
- Property taxes
- Insurance
- Owner contributions
- Owner distributions

## Rule

Financial data must be accurate, auditable, and tied to properties, units, leases, vendors, and documents.
