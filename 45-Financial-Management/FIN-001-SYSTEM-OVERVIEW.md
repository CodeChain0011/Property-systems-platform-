# FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT OVERVIEW

## Purpose

The Financial Management system tracks rent, deposits, expenses, invoices, vendor payments, owner reporting, property profitability, and financial performance.

## Goals

- Track money accurately
- Connect financial activity to properties, units, leases, vendors, and work orders
- Support reporting
- Support tax preparation
- Support owner decision-making
- Preserve audit history

## Core Areas

- Rent Collection
- Security Deposits
- Expense Tracking
- Vendor Payments
- Ledger
- Reports
- Budgeting
- Forecasting
