# VENDOR PAYMENTS

## Purpose

Track payments to contractors and service providers.

## Payment Flow

1. Vendor submits invoice.
2. Manager reviews.
3. Invoice is approved.
4. Payment is issued.
5. Financial transaction is created.
6. Vendor ledger updates.
7. Work order cost updates.

## Rule

Vendor payments must link to invoice, vendor, work order, property, and ledger.
