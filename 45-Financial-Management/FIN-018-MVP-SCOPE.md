# FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT MVP SCOPE

## Included

- Basic Rent Tracking
- Basic Expense Tracking
- Vendor Payment Records
- Security Deposit Tracking
- Rent Roll
- Financial Dashboard

## Deferred

- Full Double-Entry Accounting
- Bank Reconciliation
- Tax Filing
- Advanced Forecasting
- Automated 1099 Generation

## Rule

MVP financial system should provide useful tracking without pretending to replace accounting software.
