# RENT COLLECTION

## Purpose

Track rent charges, payments, failed payments, late fees, and balances.

## Flow

1. Lease creates rent obligation.
2. System generates charge.
3. Tenant pays.
4. Payment is processed.
5. Receipt is created.
6. Ledger updates.
7. Balance updates.

## Rule

Rent payments must connect to lease, tenant, unit, property, and ledger.
