# REPORTING AND ANALYTICS OVERVIEW

## Purpose

The Reporting and Analytics system turns operational data into useful insight for landlords, managers, owners, vendors, and executives.

## Goals

- Show operational status
- Identify risks
- Track financial performance
- Monitor maintenance
- Track occupancy
- Support compliance
- Guide decision-making

## Core Areas

- Dashboards
- Standard Reports
- Financial Reports
- Maintenance Reports
- Compliance Reports
- Portfolio Analytics
- AI Insights
