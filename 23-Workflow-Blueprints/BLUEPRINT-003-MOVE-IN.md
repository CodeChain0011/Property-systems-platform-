# BLUEPRINT 003: MOVE-IN

## Purpose

Map the move-in process.

## Flow

1. Lease is signed.
2. Deposit is confirmed.
3. Move-in date is scheduled.
4. Move-in checklist is created.
5. Access instructions are sent.
6. Inspection is performed.
7. Photos and videos are uploaded.
8. Condition record is confirmed.
9. Tenant receives welcome materials.
10. Unit status becomes occupied.

## Records Created

- Inspection
- Document
- Communication
- Event
- Audit Log

## Automation Opportunities

- Welcome packet
- Key/access reminder
- Utility setup reminder
- Move-in inspection checklist
