# ENTERPRISE SCALE ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW

## Purpose

The Enterprise Scale Architecture defines how the Property Operating System can grow from a small landlord tool into a platform that supports large portfolios, multiple organizations, multi-state operations, advanced permissions, high data volume, integrations, and enterprise reporting.

## Goals

- Support small landlords and enterprise operators with one architecture
- Support thousands of properties and units
- Support multiple companies and ownership structures
- Support multi-state compliance
- Support enterprise-grade security
- Support reporting at scale
- Support integrations and automation
- Support future AI intelligence

## Rule

Enterprise capability should be planned early but activated gradually.
