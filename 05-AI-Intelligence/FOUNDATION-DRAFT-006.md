# PROPERTY OPERATING SYSTEM

## FOUNDATION DRAFT 006

### AI and Intelligence Layer

This document defines the future intelligence layer of the platform.

## AI Objectives

The AI layer should help users understand, decide, and act.

It should not replace legal, financial, or human judgment.

## Intelligence Areas

1. Maintenance risk prediction
2. Lease renewal recommendation
3. Tenant risk indicators
4. Vendor performance scoring
5. CapEx forecasting
6. Rent optimization support
7. Compliance deadline reminders
8. Document summarization
9. Portfolio health scoring
10. Operational assistant

## AI Safety Rule

AI recommendations must always show:

- Data used
- Confidence level
- Reasoning summary
- Human approval requirement
- Legal or financial disclaimer when needed
