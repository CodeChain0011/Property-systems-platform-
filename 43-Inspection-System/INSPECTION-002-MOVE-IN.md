# MOVE-IN INSPECTION

## Purpose

Document unit condition before tenant occupancy.

## Required Capture

- Room-by-room checklist
- Photos
- Videos
- Notes
- Tenant acknowledgment
- Manager acknowledgment
- Date and time stamp

## Areas

- Walls
- Floors
- Ceilings
- Doors
- Windows
- Appliances
- Plumbing
- Electrical
- HVAC
- Fixtures
- Exterior Access

## Rule

Move-in inspection becomes the baseline condition record.
