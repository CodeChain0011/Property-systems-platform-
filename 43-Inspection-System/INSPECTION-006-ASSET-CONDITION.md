# ASSET CONDITION INSPECTION

## Purpose

Evaluate the condition of property assets.

## Assets

- HVAC
- Roof
- Water Heater
- Appliances
- Electrical Panel
- Plumbing
- Irrigation
- Security Devices

## Outputs

- Condition Rating
- Repair Recommendation
- Replacement Recommendation
- Risk Level

## Rule

Asset inspections update asset lifecycle history.
