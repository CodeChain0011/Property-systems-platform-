# VENDOR PORTAL OVERVIEW

## Purpose

The Vendor Portal gives contractors and service providers a controlled workspace for assigned work, scheduling, documentation, invoices, and communication.

## Goals

- Reduce manual coordination
- Improve repair tracking
- Collect completion evidence
- Track vendor performance
- Improve invoice accuracy
- Centralize vendor documents

## Core Areas

- Vendor Dashboard
- Work Orders
- Scheduling
- Documents
- Invoices
- Messages
- Profile
- Compliance
