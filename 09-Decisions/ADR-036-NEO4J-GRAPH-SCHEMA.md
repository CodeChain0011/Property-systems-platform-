# ADR-036 NEO4J GRAPH SCHEMA

## Decision

The Property Operating System will use Neo4j as a relationship intelligence layer alongside PostgreSQL.

## Reason

Property operations contain complex relationships between people, properties, units, leases, work orders, assets, documents, financial records, compliance rules, and events.

## Consequence

PostgreSQL remains the official system of record. Neo4j supports graph traversal, AI context, relationship discovery, risk analysis, and intelligence workflows.

## Status

Accepted
