# ADR-043 DEPLOYMENT ARCHITECTURE

## Decision

The Property Operating System will use separated local, development, staging, and production environments with repeatable deployment, backups, monitoring, and rollback planning.

## Reason

The platform handles legal, financial, tenant, document, compliance, and operational records.

## Consequence

Deployment planning must exist before production launch and must be connected to testing, security, data protection, and disaster recovery.

## Status

Accepted
