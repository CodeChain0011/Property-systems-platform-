# ADR-033 ENTERPRISE SCALE ARCHITECTURE

## Decision

The platform will be designed so it can scale from small landlords to enterprise real estate operators.

## Reason

The long-term vision requires one operating model that can support both simple and complex property businesses.

## Consequence

Multi-tenant architecture, permissions, reporting, compliance, documents, automation, integrations, and AI must be designed with future scale in mind.

## Status

Accepted
